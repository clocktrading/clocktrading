ALTCOINS
In 2011, rival cryptocurrencies emerged. As Bitcoin gained popularity and the idea of decentralized and encrypted currencies caught on, the first alternative cryptocurrencies emerged. These other Cryptocurrencies are sometimes called altcoins and generally try to improve on the original Bitcoin design by offering greater speed, anonymity or some other advantage. Among the first to emerge were Namecoin and Litecoin. Currently there are over 1,000 cryptocurrencies in circulation with new ones frequently appearing.
